Detailed Description

The stars are arranged in triangular zones according to StelGeodesicGrid. Bright stars (Star1) are stored in zones with small level, whereas fainter stars (Star2,Star3) are stored in zones with higher level. A ZoneData contains stars of a given type (Star1,Star2 or Star3) of a given triangle and level of the StelGeodesicGrid.
