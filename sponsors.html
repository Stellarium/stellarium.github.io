Supporters and friends of Stellarium

Stellarium is produced by the efforts of the developer team, with the help and support of the following people and organisations:

Sponsors

  • Sourceforge for hosting the project files, website and SVN repositories. Sourceforge have pushed many terabytes of data for Stellarium during the life of the project.
  • Launchpad for hosting the trackers.
  • The European Southern Observatory for sponsoring and hosting the 2007 Stellarium developer meeting.

Community

Special thanks go out to all members of the Stellarium user community.

  • Program translators
  • User guide translators
  • Landscape authors
  • Wiki authors
  • Testers and bug reporters
  • Donators
