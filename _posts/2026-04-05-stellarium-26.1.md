Glad to announce the release of Stellarium 26.1 for all platforms. This is our first release in 2026 with exciting features and many 
bug fixes.

The major changes of this version:
- Added two new sky cultures
- Added Time Navigator plugin
- Many improvements for support Windows/arm64
- Many significant improvements in sky cultures support
- Many improvements in plugins
- Many improvements in Core and GUI
- Many updates in sky cultures

Thanks to all the developers, testers, and users who made this release possible with your contributions and feedback.
